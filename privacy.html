Last updated: February 4, 2026

Overview

Shuqin Wu Studio (“we,” “our,” or “us”) creates casual mobile games for the App Store. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information.

Information We Collect

  • Gameplay data such as progress, session length, and feature usage.
  • Support requests you submit through email.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve our games and services.
  • Respond to support inquiries and player feedback.
  • Ensure stability, security, and quality of the experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited data with service providers that help us operate the apps, only when necessary and under appropriate safeguards.

Data Retention

We keep information only for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

Children’s Privacy

Our games are designed to be family-friendly. If you believe we have collected personal information from a child, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Your Choices

You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data where applicable.
